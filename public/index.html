Products

Three focused offers for teams that need AI to be useful, visible, and safe.

SecureRAG Gateway

Privacy-preserving infrastructure for companies exposing AI APIs to the public, including automotive agents, finance assistants, healthcare LLMs, and enterprise support bots.

  • Protect RAG datastores from extraction and probing attacks.
  • Guard tool calls, logs, and returned context against PII leakage.
  • Add retrieval policy, redaction, risk scoring, and audit trails.

AI Search Growth

SEO and answer-engine optimization for teams that want their pages to be found when people ask ChatGPT, Gemini, and other AI search systems for resources.

  • Rewrite pages for clear entities, topics, snippets, and citations.
  • Create AI-readable indexes, structured metadata, and crawl paths.
  • Track discoverability and improve pages with measurable tests.

CareerLift Agent

A multi-model agent that improves CVs, resumes, statements, cover letters, and application packages for jobs, internships, graduate school, and scholarships.

  • Tailors materials to role, program, rubric, and applicant history.
  • Checks consistency, missing proof, weak claims, and tone.
  • Produces polished drafts while preserving the applicant's voice.
