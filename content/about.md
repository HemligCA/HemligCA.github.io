Hemlig turns AI privacy and agent research into commercial products.

We work with companies that expose AI APIs, RAG systems, and tool-calling agents to public users, where privacy failures can become customer, compliance, and brand risks. We also help teams improve AI-search visibility and provide a multi-model agent for stronger CVs, resumes, statements, and application packages.

Our team combines PhD-level research experience with practical product engineering. The result is focused implementation: privacy controls that fit real systems, content strategy that improves discoverability, and agents that help users reach concrete outcomes.
